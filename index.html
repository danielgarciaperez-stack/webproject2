Dream Trip to Japan

Japan is at the top of my travel list. I want to visit in spring to see the cherry blossoms, try local ramen, and explore both quiet temples and bright city streets.

Places I Want to Visit

  • Tokyo – Shibuya Crossing and Tokyo Tower
  • Kyoto – Kinkaku-ji and Fushimi Inari Shrine
  • Nara – Deer Park and Todai-ji Temple
  • Mount Fuji – lakes and mountain views
  • Osaka – street food and night life

Trip Cost and Timing

Month Estimated Cost Notes
